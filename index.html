WELCOME

Thanks to almost 30 years, we understand the business of engineered fabric structures, our customers’ needs, and pride ourselves on the dedication we show.That’s why more businesses, households, Public Works professionals, and wholesalers across North America trust Calhoun Super Structure to deliver engineered tension fabric structures solutions that will help them meet their needs .

If you're looking for a more efficient storage space, event venue, production facility, or livestock housing, our high-level engineered fabric structures are the solution. Fabric-covered building systems offer numerous benefits such as high durability, energy efficiency, and easy installation, resulting in a stronger, longer-lasting structure suited for any industry.At Calhoun Super Structure, we pride ourselves on providing our customers with fabric structures that reflect our unique engineering standards at a competitive cost. Whether you're looking to store equipment, house livestock, or anything in between, our fabric-covered buildings can meet your needs. Our fabric building systems can endure even the harshest weather conditions, keeping your critical assets safe. You also instantly save on electricity costs from the natural light of the fabric versus traditional wood or steel buildings.
